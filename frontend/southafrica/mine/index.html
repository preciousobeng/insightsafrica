About
Sentinel-2 optical imagery (10m resolution). NDVI tracks vegetation health - loss indicates land clearing or mining activity. NDWI tracks water body changes - decline indicates contamination or mine drainage. Covers formal operations (Witwatersrand, Bushveld, Limpopo) and illegal zama zama sites (Gauteng/North West). Data from ESA Copernicus (free).
