About
CHIRPS (Climate Hazards Group InfraRed Precipitation with Station data) — 0.05° (~5km) resolution daily rainfall. Monitors flash flood risk across Cape Verde's 9 inhabited islands via ribeiras (narrow volcanic gorges). Data: USGS + NOAA (free). Updated daily.
